TIFFR

Code / Interaction / Design

fightingtheboss

Visit

TIFFR is a planning companion for the Toronto International Film Festival that helps people shortlist, schedule and share their festival. Shortlisting films is made easy by using our bookmarklet when you find a film you'd like to see on the official TIFF website. The festival planner allows users to see all of the screenings available for their shortlisted films and select the ones that work for them.

Launched in 2009, the site was my first experience with Ruby on Rails, fluid layouts and application-level javascript.
